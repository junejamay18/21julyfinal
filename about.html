Nurtured by Nature, Formulated by Botanists

At Luminary Botanicals, we harvest organic plants at peak vitality to craft bio-active serums, herbal teas, and soothing body balms designed to nourish body and mind.

Cold-Pressed Integrity & Wild Harvests

Founded in Northern California, Luminary Botanicals was established on the belief that real skincare stems from unadulterated plant biochemistry.

We work directly with sustainable family farms and certified organic foragers. By eschewing high-heat processing and synthetic preservatives, our formulations retain their natural antioxidant profiles and vibrant botanical aromas.
