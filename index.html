Cold-Pressed Purity & Sustainable Sourcing

Every oil, essence, and elixir from Luminary Botanicals is extracted using low-temperature methods to preserve bioactive nutrients, vitamins, and living enzymes.

Zero Synthetic Additives

We strictly avoid parabens, sulfates, artificial fragrances, mineral oils, and synthetic fillers. Our preservative systems use radish root ferment and natural rosemary extracts.

Violet Glass Protection

Our formulations are bottled in light-blocking biophotonic amber and Miron violet glass containers. This preserves molecular potency without chemical stabilizers.

Carbon Neutral Courier Shipping

All packaging components, including our soy-based inks and hemp twine, are 100% compostable and shipped via carbon-offset postal routes.
